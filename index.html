Global Water Distribution

Only 3% of Earth's water is freshwater, and only 0.5% is available for drinking. The rest is locked in glaciers, ice caps, and underground.

Daily Water Usage

The average person uses about 135 litres of water per day. In India, urban households use approximately 150-200 litres daily per person.

Leaky Pipes Matter

A dripping faucet can waste more than 11,000 litres per year. That's enough water for over 180 showers!

Agriculture Impact

Agriculture accounts for 70% of global freshwater use. It takes about 2,500 litres of water to produce just one kilogram of rice.

Energy Connection

Heating water accounts for about 18% of home energy use. Conserving hot water saves both water and energy.

Indoor vs Outdoor

About 30% of household water is used outdoors. In dry climates, outdoor use can account for up to 60% of total consumption.
